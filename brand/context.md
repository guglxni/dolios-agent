# Dolios Brand Context

## Taglines (use by context)
- **Primary** (under wordmark): "The Crafty Agent"
- **Marketing / Hero**: "Scheme. Execute. Deliver."
- **Long-form / Blog**: "Hermes' Sharpest Heir"
- **Enterprise / Pitch**: "Autonomous intelligence, mythological precision."

## Color Palette
- Deep Midnight (#1A1A2E) — 60% usage, backgrounds, authority
- Signal Red (#E94560) — 10% usage, CTAs, action indicators
- Strategy Blue (#0F3460) — 25% usage, secondary text, code, links
- Dark Navy (#16213E) — gradients, sidebars, cards
- Antiquity Gold (#C4A35A) — 5% usage, premium accents, Greek letterforms
- Light Gray (#F5F5F7) — page backgrounds, inputs

## Typography
- Display: Playfair Display (serif, 700 weight)
- Body: Inter (sans-serif, 400-500 weight)
- Code: JetBrains Mono (monospace, 400 weight)
- Greek accents: Noto Serif (0.06em letter-spacing)

## Logo System
- Pictorial Mark: Greek Delta (Δ) with internal node graph
- Lettermark: Geometric "D" with Signal Red arrow
- Wordmark: "DOLIOS" in Playfair Display with labyrinth O's
- Lockup: Delta icon + wordmark + "THE CRAFTY AGENT"

## Voice Attributes
- Cunning without being smug
- Precise without being cold
- Grounded in mythology without being forced
- Technical without being exclusionary
